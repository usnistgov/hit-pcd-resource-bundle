About the tool

Welcome to the Integrating the Healthcare Enterprise (IHE) Patient Care Device (PCD) Testing Tool.

This tool was developed by the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) to support testing of the IHE PCD Test Cases for Health Level 7 (HL7) Version 2 (v2).

User registration is recommended but not required; you can use the tool as a "guest" user. However registration is required to save your customized settings. Click-on "Register" in the upper-right corner menu of this page for self-registration.
